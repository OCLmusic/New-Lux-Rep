About

Jesus Lopez has spent over two decades painting some of the finest homes in the Vail Valley. His passion for painting is reflected in every project and has earned him a reputation for precision, quality, and reliability.

Luxury Painting Inc provides premium residential and commercial painting, with a focus on careful preparation, attention to detail, and clean execution. Every project is thoughtfully planned and executed to deliver lasting results and a seamless experience for our clients.

Together with his son Ossiel, Jesus leads a team dedicated to delivering high-end finishes and a painting experience that exceeds expectations.
