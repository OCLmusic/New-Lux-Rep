2-Year Workmanship Warranty

Every project is backed by our comprehensive two-year warranty covering all labor and workmanship.

What Our Warranty Covers

Our warranty provides protection against defects in workmanship, including:

  • Peeling or Flaking: If paint peels or flakes due to improper application or preparation, we'll make it right
  • Blistering: Coverage for blistering caused by application issues
  • Cracking: Protection against cracking resulting from workmanship defects
  • Uneven Coverage: Any areas with inconsistent coverage will be corrected
  • Color Inconsistency: We ensure uniform color throughout your project

Warranty Conditions

To maintain warranty coverage, please note the following conditions:

  • The warranty applies to the original customer and property
  • Coverage begins on the date of project completion
  • Normal wear and tear is not covered
  • Damage caused by accidents, alterations, or neglect is not covered
  • Issues resulting from structural movement, settling, or water intrusion from external sources are not covered
  • Exterior surfaces are subject to Colorado's extreme weather conditions; some fading and weathering over time is normal and not considered a defect

How to Make a Warranty Claim

If you experience any issues covered under our warranty, we make the resolution process simple:

  • Contact Us: Call us at (970) 470-5533 or email info@luxurypaintinginc.com
  • Describe the Issue: Provide details about the problem and, if possible, photos of the affected area
  • Schedule an Inspection: We'll arrange a convenient time to assess the situation
  • Resolution: If the issue is covered under warranty, we'll complete the necessary repairs at no cost to you

We respond to all warranty inquiries within 48 hours and strive to resolve covered issues as quickly as possible.
